About Us

BSERI was born on the strong realization of the acute need for a full-fledged MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS TRAINING INSTITUTE by Govind Srinivasan, the Founder-Director of BSERI, based on his close association with Management System Standards (MSS) practice, for more than a decade.


Mission & Purpose

BSERI’s focus is to conduct MSS IMPLEMENTATION TRAINING COURSES, covering almost all the popular certifiable MSS. The acute deficit of MSS implementation skills is the primary requirement that BSERI recognizes and aims to fulfil in its long journey helping organizations that require solid implementation expertise in different MSS. BSERI would like to elevate MSS Training into an entirely different dimension that can facilitate comprehensive and involved learning, as opposed to minimal learning.


BSERI envisions to engage itself in RESEARCH on MSS and the associated areas of knowledge and using these research findings in making continual improvement of the MSS Training Strategy, methods, techniques and related offerings
